Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how the Invoice Extractor desktop app ("App") handles data when used by authorized Diesel Power Products users.

Information We Access

  • Gmail message metadata and content needed to identify invoice emails.
  • Email attachments required for invoice extraction and processing.
  • Basic Google account profile details used to confirm the authorized account.

How We Use Information

  • Download invoice attachments from the authorized mailbox.
  • Parse invoice data and generate spreadsheet output for accounting workflows.
  • Prevent use of unauthorized Google accounts in the App.

Data Storage

  • The App stores OAuth tokens locally on the user device.
  • Processed files are saved locally and/or on company-managed storage.
  • The App is not intended as a public cloud service.

Data Sharing

Data processed by the App is used for internal business operations and is not sold to third parties.

Security

Access is limited by Google authentication, company account controls, and local machine access controls.
